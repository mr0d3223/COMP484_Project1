INTRO

Hello! My name is Martin Rodriguez. This website will introduce you to some of my favorite things.

I am 27 years old and this is my 5th year at CSUN. I was in the Marine Corps for 5 years. I went straight to boot camp 2 days after I graduated high school in 2016 at Grover Cleveland High School.
Thanks to being in the Marine Corps I was able to travel the world, visiting 15 different countries and living in Japan for 2 years. This is where I developed my passion for traveling, and I’ve made it a mission to travel at least twice a year and try to visit Japan every year.
After enrolling in school I was fortunate enough to get a pet dog named Asuna. She is an Australian Shepherd and honestly feels like my child. She’s almost 3 years old and has had 2 litters with my roommate’s dog Tato (short for Potato).
I love movies, especially cheesy B-rated horror films. I’m not sure why, but they always make me laugh. I also enjoy learning new hobbies—so far I’ve picked up crocheting, making miniature paper theater art, gardening, and growing bonsai trees I love being in the outdoors, ill take any chance to go on a hike when i travel or go to the ocean for a quick swim.
